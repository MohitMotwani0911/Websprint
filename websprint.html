WebSprint: Web Development Bootcamp & Competition

M Block,, IET DAVV, Indore, 452020 GDG on Campus Institute of Engineering and Technology, DAVV - Indore, India WebSprint Bootcamp & Competition is an intensive, hands-on event designed to empower participants with essential web development skills and put their abilities to the test through a real-world competition. The bootcamp covers web technologies, culminating in a challenge where teams build functional web applications. It is a perfect opportunity for students and tech enthusiasts to learn and grow.

Event Overview

WebSprint is an exciting and engaging event designed to equip participants with essential web development skills, while encouraging innovation and creativity. The event is structured in two phases: the first phase is a detailed bootcamp, where industry professionals and mentors guide participants through both foundational and advanced web development topics. Attendees gain practical experience with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, and more, covering both front-end and back-end aspects. The second phase is the competition, where participants team up to tackle real-world challenges by building fully functional web applications. This phase emphasizes problem-solving, creativity, and teamwork, with a focus on producing clean code, intuitive user interfaces, and high-performance applications. Key Highlights: 1.Skill-Boosting Bootcamp:** Dive into modern web technologies through instructor-led sessions2)Exciting Competition:** Put your skills to the test by developing a web app from the ground up. 2.Tech Stack:** Work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, APIs, and deployment tools. 3.Awards & Recognition:** Top performers will receive attractive prizes, certificates, and valuable networking opportunities. The WebSprint Bootcamp & Competition is the ideal platform for students, developers, and coding enthusiasts eager to sharpen their web development abilities, collaborate with peers, and challenge themselves in a competitive yet supportive atmosphere.
